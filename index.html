Our Goal

Our program intends to connect community members with each other. This interactive social media platform removes toxic metrics such as likes, views, and followers to focus purely on the aspects of image and video sharing. We aim to help people feel closer and less lonely, without the harmful comparing associated with other media-sharing platforms.

Key Features

  • Personal accounts incorporate password security to protect user identities.
  • Augmented reality is used to create an immersive media-consumption platform.
  • Keep in touch (literally!) with AR technology with your friends and family.
